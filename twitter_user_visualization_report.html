Conclusions and Summary of Results¶

From the charts above, we can infer that this twitter account has received a steady increase in audience engagements as the number of retweets and likes enjoyed a gradual increase for the period under review

Additionally, it is observed that there is a very strong and positive correlation between the number of retweets and the number of likes each tweet gets. The higher the retweet, the higher the likes.

Furthermore, it can be seem that the most breeds of dogs featured in the tweets we reviewed are the Golden Retriever, Labrador Retriever, Pembroke, Chihuahua and Pug. It was also observed that all the above mentioned breeds of dogs were the dogs with the highest ratings, most retweets and most likes except for the Pug which was replaced by the Samoyed breed in the top 5.

In conclusion, this has been a worthwhile project in understanding the entire process of data wrangling and creating visualizations from which meaningful insights can be drawn
